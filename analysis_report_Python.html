Summary

We rate Dogs is a very interesting twitter account. They rate dogs out of 10 and most of the dogs get rating more than 10. Sometimes, they give funny rating such as on the independence day. The tweets have been retweeted and liked as a favorite a lot. Most tweets that are retweeted more are also liked more, as is evident from the high correlation between the 2 factors. Most tweets are posted through Iphone app of twitter. Some others that are used are Tweet deck and tweet web client.
